The Framework

Four Distinct Social Styles

Everyone falls into one of four communication styles based on how assertive and responsive they are. Which one are you?

Driver

Task-focused and assertive. Values results, efficiency, and direct action. Decisive leaders who get things done.

Expressive

People-focused and assertive. Values recognition, creativity, and social connection. Enthusiastic and inspiring.

Amiable

People-focused and collaborative. Values cooperation, harmony, and security. Supportive team players.

Analytical

Task-focused and methodical. Values accuracy, logic, and thorough analysis. Careful and precise thinkers.
