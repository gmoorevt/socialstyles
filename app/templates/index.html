What is a Social Style?

Your Social Style is determined by two key dimensions: Assertiveness and Responsiveness. The assessment measures these dimensions to identify your primary style.

The Four Social Styles

Analytical

Asks & Controls

Analyticals are logical, thorough, and serious. They value facts, organization, and careful analysis before making decisions.

Driver

Tells & Controls

Drivers are direct, decisive, and results-oriented. They focus on tasks, efficiency, and getting things done quickly.

Amiable

Asks & Emotes

Amiables are supportive, cooperative, and relationship-focused. They value harmony, stability, and helping others.

Expressive

Tells & Emotes

Expressives are enthusiastic, creative, and people-oriented. They enjoy socializing, sharing ideas, and inspiring others.
